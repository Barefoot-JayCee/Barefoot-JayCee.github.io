About Me

Hi, I’m JayCee! My journey has taken me from military service to trades like welding and electrical work, to owning a small remodeling business, and even working in insurance. I grew up valuing hard work and craftsmanship, but I’ve always been drawn to tech development and operations. Thanks to a fantastic group of friends who encouraged me, I finally took the plunge into web development, and it’s been an exciting ride ever since. I’m passionate about creating impactful and sustainable digital solutions. My work is guided by Stoicism, focusing on contributing to the greater good and making a positive impact on the future. I offer affordable websites to small businesses that prioritize social and environmental causes, and sometimes, I do it for free if I see a cause that truly needs my help. Outside of work, you’ll find me hiking, camping, reading philosophy and history, or working on DIY projects. I love learning and tackling new challenges every day. Let’s connect and see how we can make a difference together!
